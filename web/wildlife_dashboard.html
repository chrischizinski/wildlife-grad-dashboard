About the Graduate Position Intelligence Dashboard

🎓 Graduate Position Intelligence Platform

This dashboard transforms wildlife job posting data into actionable insights specifically for graduate students. Unlike general job boards, we use machine learning to identify and analyze only graduate opportunities, providing you with accurate market intelligence for your academic career planning.

📊 How It Works
  • Automated collection of wildlife job postings
  • ML classification to identify graduate positions
  • Real-time analytics focused on graduate opportunities
  • Transparent reporting of classification success rates
🔍 Data Sources

Data collected from wildlife and fisheries job postings and processed through machine learning classification algorithms. All metrics shown reflect graduate positions only.
